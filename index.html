ABOUT THE BAND

Glass Machines plays intense instrumental music with shades of funk, jazz and jamming. Glass Machines formed in March 2013 with the goal of producing the biggest live sound possible with three people.

Gary, Mike and Matt are experienced musicians from southern New England. They've studied music formally at URI. They've taught private music lessons. They've played in Providence-area bands like Nights Without Television, Something Red, and The Smiths Cover Band. They've been jamming hard from day one.
